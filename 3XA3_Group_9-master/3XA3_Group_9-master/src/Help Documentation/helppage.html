Ratava Help

Welcome to Ratava! Follow the instructions below to begin

Step 1: Enter your prefered hash in the "Input hash" Text field

Step 2: Enter the name of your output file. It is not necessary to add the .jpg extention

Note: You can choose to specify the location of your output file. If not specified, it will be generated in the current directory

Step 3 (Optional): Enter the colour you wish to fix for the body colour. It must be a hex colour value

Step 4 (Optional): Enter the colour you wish to fix for the accessories colour. It must be a hex colour value

Step 5 (Optional): Enter the hash encoding you wish to use. The default hash is sha256

Step 6: Click the generate button to generate your avatar. It will automatically open in the default image viewing program on your computer
